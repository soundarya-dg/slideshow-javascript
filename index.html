Wonders of the World
Our world is filled with the most unique structures that are both man-made and natural.
Some of the man-made creations include churches, tombs, temples, monuments, mosques, buildings and even cities.
At present, the Seven Wonders of the World are the Taj Mahal, the Colosseum, the Chichen Itza, Machu Picchu, Christ the Redeemer, Petra, and the Great Wall of China.
